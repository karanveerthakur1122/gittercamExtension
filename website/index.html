Features

Everything you need to manage your camera

One extension replaces your webcam feed with effects the meeting app can't distinguish from a real camera.

❄

Freeze Frame

Hold the current frame and walk away. The meeting sees you sitting naturally.

↻

Loop Recording

Record 3-10 seconds of yourself, then replay it on loop while you're gone.

⚡

Bad Connection

Simulate a laggy, stuttering feed. Buy yourself time without anyone suspecting.

🎨

Color Filters

Apply B&W, Sepia, Warm, Cool, or Vivid filters to your live camera feed.

🎙

Audio Effects

Voice changer, ambient room tone, or fake typing sounds mixed into your mic.

⏰

Scheduled Freeze

Set a timer to automatically freeze your camera after a delay, then resume.

🛡

Screen Share Safe

Effects auto-disable when you share your screen. The overlay hides too.

🚨

Panic Button

One key (Alt+P) instantly reverts everything to live camera and real mic.

📌

Floating Overlay

A draggable in-page widget to switch modes without opening the popup.

Architecture

How it works

GitterCam intercepts the camera stream before the meeting app sees it.

Real Camera → getUserMedia patch (inject.js)
                ↓
      pipeline.js — canvas engine (freeze / loop / stutter / filters)
                ↓ canvas.captureStream()
      Meeting App — sees GitterCam output as the camera

Real Mic → audio-pipeline.js (Web Audio graph)
                ↓
      Meeting App — receives processed audio track
